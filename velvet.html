Welcome to the Velvet Room!

Overview

Welcome to the home page of my project. I chose to make a small database for my favorite game Persona 3. I took my information and pictures from the Megami Tensei Wiki and do not claim to own any of the assets used in making this site. This is nothing more than passion project.(Which i just so happen to be making before turning it into a CSC 225 project.) I plan to add more to this project in the future.

At the top of the screen you can see navigation buttons to move from this home page to the Persona 3 page and the form page. On the Persona 3 page you will see an overview of the plot, a description of each character and their persona as well as the skills that persona can learn. The form page will allow you to fill out a quick form as the name implies
